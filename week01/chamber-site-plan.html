Site Plan

Site Name

Lima Chamber of Commerce. This name is from the city where I live, so I will make the chamber of commerce of this place.

Purpose

The Lima Chamber of Commerce website aims to
-Provide information about tourist places in Lima, giving a showcase to our partners in order to attract customers to affiliated businesses.
-Make the list of our partners' businesses freely available to the general public.

Scenarios

What training do you provide this month?
How much is the monthly cost that I must pay to join the chamber of commerce?
What accommodations, restaurants and other businesses can I book in here in Lima?
